Ebb and Flow
The Vandalism Tide of Portland

Discover the dynamics of vandalism in Portland, Oregon, from 2015 to 2023. This interactive map visualizes annual changes in vandalism across Portland neighborhoods using proportional symbols and a time slider, providing a clear, engaging overview of trends. Powered by data from the Portland Police Bureau, VandalMap offers a unique, user-friendly tool for community leaders, policymakers, and residents to explore and understand the spatial and temporal patterns of urban vandalism. Dive into Portland's neighborhoods like never before, and empower your community with insights for a safer tomorrow.

Please exercise caution and critical judgment when engaging with the data, keeping in mind the need for further verification. Contributions and corrections are warmly invited to enhance the accuracy and comprehensiveness of this resource.
